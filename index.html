Pompompurin

He is a golden retriever dog, introduced in 1996. His birthday is Apirl 16th and he lives in a basket. He is very goofy and loves to sleep.

Pompompurin loves to eat. he is the one of the chubbiest sanrio characters. His favourite food is caramel pudding. His name Purin comes from the Romaji for pudding in japanese.

Kuromi

kuromi is a rabbit in a black jester hat, introduced in 2005. her birthday is on October 31. she is my melodys rival.

she is a icon for punk, emo, scene and goth crowds, however she is every girly. She loves to write in her diary and loves romantic short stories. Kuromi also leads a biker gang named the Kuromi's 5

Keroppi

keroppi is a frog, introduced in 1988. his birthday is on july 10th. he lives in a house by donut pond.

Keroppi lives with his family, his brother, sister and parents. he likes to play games with his friends, he loves playing baseketball and boomerangs

Badtz-Maru

Badtz-Maru is a penguin with spiky hair, born on apirl 1st in hawaii, introduced in 1993. He lives in Gorgeoustown with his parents and younger tiwn brothers.

Badtz-Maru is very mischievous, he takes his pet alligator on walks gaining him lots of attraction. Badtz-Maru runs the XO clan, which comes from his name, badtz meaning X and maru means O in Japanese. X-O signify wrong-correct.

My Melody

My Melody is hello kitty's best friend. Born on january 18th, introduced in 1976. she portrays little red riding hood, hense the hood.

My melodys favourite thing is baking cookies with her mom, her favourite food is almond pound cake. she is very honest and kind-hearted. Kuromi likes the idea of having a rival more than disliking melody, so neither of them actualy dislike eachother.

Cinnamroll

Cinnamroll is a white fluffy puppy. he was born on march 6th, introduced in 2001. he works at cafe cinnamon and will sleep on customers laps.

Cinnamroll favourite food is indeed a nice warm cinnabun. He was born in the clouds but fell down to the ground where he met the owner of cinnamon cafe. He can be quite shy but is very friendly. He can also fly using his big ears as wings.
